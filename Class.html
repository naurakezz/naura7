Classes Offered at Vixen Global Academy

Technology

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Explore the basics of AI, including machine learning, neural networks, and deep learning. Learn how AI is shaping the future of technology.

Web Development

Master the fundamentals of front-end and back-end web development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React and Node.js.

Cybersecurity Principles

Understand how to protect digital systems from attacks and secure sensitive information through hands-on labs and theoretical knowledge.

Business

Business Management 101

Learn the foundational principles of managing a business, including leadership, strategy, operations, and organizational structure.

Marketing in the Digital Age

Discover modern marketing techniques, including digital and social media marketing, SEO, content marketing, and consumer behavior.

Entrepreneurship and Innovation

Gain insights into the world of startups and innovation, covering topics such as product development, funding, and scaling a business.

Arts

Graphic Design Fundamentals

Develop your creative skills by learning about design principles, typography, color theory, and software tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Photography and Visual Storytelling

Explore the art of photography and learn how to tell compelling stories through images using composition, lighting, and editing techniques.

Film and Media Production

Learn the process of creating films and media content, from scriptwriting and directing to editing and post-production.

Science

Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering

Understand the key concepts and applications of biotechnology and genetic engineering in fields such as medicine, agriculture, and environmental science.

Environmental Science and Sustainability

Learn about the impact of human activity on the environment and explore solutions for creating a more sustainable future.

Physics of the Universe

Dive into the fundamentals of astrophysics, exploring the nature of the universe, stars, black holes, and cosmological phenomena.
