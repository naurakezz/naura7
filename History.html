History of Vixen Global Academy

Our Founding

Vixen Global Academy was founded in 1990 by a group of visionary educators and industry leaders who shared a common goal: to create a modern institution of higher learning that would prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century. The founders believed in a forward-thinking curriculum that combined practical skills with innovative approaches to education.

From its humble beginnings as a small learning center with only 100 students, Vixen Global Academy quickly grew into one of the most respected universities in New York City. The institution was built on the values of excellence, diversity, and global impact, and these values have guided its growth and success ever since.

Our Growth

Over the years, Vixen Global Academy expanded its academic programs and its campus facilities, attracting students from across the globe. In 2005, the university introduced its first graduate programs in technology and business, marking a major milestone in its development.

By 2010, the academy had become a leader in innovation, with cutting-edge research centers and partnerships with leading companies and organizations around the world. The university also launched several international exchange programs, allowing students to study abroad and gain valuable global experience.

Our Mission Today

Today, Vixen Global Academy remains committed to its founding mission of providing a world-class education that equips students with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in an increasingly interconnected world. The university now offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs, with a focus on technology, business, science, and the arts.

As we look to the future, Vixen Global Academy continues to evolve, embracing new technologies and methodologies to ensure that our students are prepared to lead and innovate in their respective fields.
